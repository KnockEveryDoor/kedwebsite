Getting Started

To get started with the #KnockEveryDoor program, watch the below video to get oriented.

Once you’ve watched the video you should register to attend one of the #KnockEveryDoor Introductory Calls.

The calls last for about an hour and have a Q&A section at the end. We’ll go over everything you need to know to get to work organizing in your community, and you’ll get to know other volunteers. After the call, we’ll be able to follow up with you individually to make sure you have all the resources we have to offer in order to get to work knocking on doors.

You can also check out the #KnockEveryDoor Video Library for some more information on the different types of canvasses we can train you on.

Once you’re ready to host a canvassing event, you can create your event here.

If you’re interested in joining one of our special volunteer teams that help support the thousands of #KnockEveryDoor volunteers hitting the streets around the country, you can do that here.

If you have any questions feel free to email us at team@knockeverydoor.org and we’ll make sure you get the information, training and support you need.
