#KnockEveryDoor New Volunteer Calls

Thanks for signing up to volunteer with #KnockEveryDoor! The next step is to register for one of our upcoming new volunteer calls using the link corresponding to the time you’re available below.

The calls last for about an hour and have a Q&A section at the end. We’ll go over everything you need to know to get to work organizing in your community, and you’ll get to know other volunteers.

If you have any questions before the call, or can’t make any of the upcoming calls, feel free to email us at team@knockeverydoor.org and we’ll make sure you get the information, training and support you need.
